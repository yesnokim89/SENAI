Child with Unique Needs

Special Education (also known as special-needs education, aided education, exceptional education, special ed. or SPED) is
the practice of educating students in a way that addresses their individual differences and special needs.

Ideally, this process involves the individually planned and systematically monitored arrangement of teaching procedures, adapted equipment and
materials, and accessible settings. These interventions are designed to help individuals with special needs achieve a higher level of personal
self-sufficiency and success in school and in their community which may not be available if the student were only given access to a typical classroom education.
